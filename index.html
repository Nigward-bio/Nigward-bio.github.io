About Me

I am the biggest skid on earth, and I am currently Calculating... old! Speaking of reaching for the stars (sometimes literally!), my Python code, developed with Mission Space Lab program, has officially achieved FLIGHT STATUS! It's pretty wild to think my code will be running on International Space Station (ISS) between April and May 2025. We're expecting the mission data and our team certificates around June 2025. Beyond space missions, I'll soon be getting an Anycubic Kobra S1 3D printer and plan to live stream my prints. And yes, I'm also a huge fan of movies and manga.

I'm currently studying Mechanical Engineering. I'm getting into lockpicking, and I know a little bit of Lua, Java, C++, Python, DuckyScript, and HTML.

My goal is to become a better programmer and engineer, creating useful tools and innovative projects. When I'm not dreaming about space or tinkering with code, you might find me trying to convince my 3D printer that it, too, can reach for the stars (or at least print a perfect Benchy on the first try!).
